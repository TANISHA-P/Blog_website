Just a Tinge About Me :)

I exist in two worlds: the real world, where everything operates in accordance with the laws of physics; and the other world, the world whose limits know no edge, where anything is possible and yet it is bound within the paradigms of my brain.

From writing stories about how the planets' running race was responsible for today's arrangement of the planets in our solar system, to writing about how our reality is but our perspective of this divine universe, my love for writing has only grown.

When I initially learned that anyone could create their own website and post it to the internet, my brain was unable to process it. It felt like, I am equipped with the most ferocious weapons I can do anything with. I had the impression that I could control everything in the universe, that I can do possibly anything, that I can unite my second world with the first...

This blog website is extremely close to my heart. I hope everyone who reads this strives to use their thoughts, senses, and imaginations to bring their worlds together here.

Loads of Love ♥

Tanisha.
